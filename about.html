About Me:

I'm Niko. I am a first generation American, raised in Seattle with Greek roots. I enjoy sports, video games, cooking, coffee, and spending time with my family. I grew up playing and watching soccer and will always root for the Sounders and Olympiakos. My favorite video game series is The Legend of Zelda. My favorite traditional Greek dish to make is Spanakopita and I'm told my makaronia me kima (Greek meat sauce with pasta) could rival my moms. I recently started baking and my salted chocolate chip cookies consistently get rave reviews. I enjoy making a good espresso.

I attended the University of Washington where I received my BA in Communication. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, where I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ES6, React, jQuery, AJAX, JSON, Ejs, Node, Express, SQL, NoSQL, PostgreSQL, Sequelize, Python, Django, MongoDB, Mongoose, Git, Github.

I'm a Full Stack Software developer. I enjoy building tools that make peoples lives easier and creating progressive web apps utilizing minimalist design for a better user experience.
